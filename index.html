Where to find Player Tag?

To get your Player Tag that you need to enter into the form above, follow the steps. There are two possible ways:

Option 1:

  • - Open the Boom Beach.
  • - Go to your home base.
  • - Tap at the top left corner, at your "username".
  • - Tooltip with "Player tag: #xxxxxxxxxx" will appear.

Option 2:

  • - Open the Boom Beach.
  • - Go to the map.
  • - Find one of your Resource bases and go inside.
  • - Tap at the top left corner, where it says "Your Resource base".
  • - Tooltip with "Player tag: #xxxxxxxxxx" will appear.
